NOVA Center for the Performing Arts welcomes performers of all experience levels. Whether you're a seasoned actor or trying theatre for the first time, we provide a supportive environment where you can grow and shine.

What to Expect at Auditions

  • Bring a prepared monologue (1-2 minutes) or be ready to cold read from the script
  • Dress comfortably and be prepared to move
  • Arrive 15 minutes early to fill out paperwork
  • Callbacks will be posted within 48 hours
