NOVA Center Legacy

NOVA Center for the Performing Arts has been a cornerstone of Billings' cultural landscape, bringing exceptional theatrical experiences to the community year after year. Our journey reflects a commitment to artistic excellence, education, and community engagement.

From intimate dramas to grand musicals, from innovative improv comedy to classical opera, NOVA has consistently provided a platform for local talent and touring productions alike. Our youth conservatory has nurtured the next generation of performers, while our main stage productions have entertained thousands of audience members.
