Join us for our annual gala celebration featuring performances, silent auction, dinner, and dancing. This elegant evening supports NOVA's mission to bring exceptional theatre to our community.

## About the Evening

The NOVAture Gala is NOVA's signature fundraising event of the year. Enjoy an unforgettable night at The Northern Hotel with:

- **Live music** live jazz band
- **Silent auction** featuring unique experiences and local art
- **Dinner and dancing** in an elegant ballroom setting
- **Special recognition** of supporters and volunteers

All proceeds directly support NOVA's productions, education programs, and community outreach.
