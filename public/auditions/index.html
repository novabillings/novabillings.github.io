Join Our Theatre Family

NOVA Center for the Performing Arts welcomes performers of all experience levels. Whether you're a seasoned actor or trying theatre for the first time, we provide a supportive environment where you can grow and shine.

What to Expect at Auditions

  • Bring a prepared monologue (1-2 minutes) or be ready to cold read from the script
  • Dress comfortably and be prepared to move
  • Arrive 15 minutes early to fill out paperwork
  • Callbacks will be posted within 48 hours

Questions?

Contact our executive director at producer@novabillings.org

Or call us at 406-591-9535

As You Like It

By William Shakespeare

Directed by Glenda Brauneis

As You Like It, follows Rosalind, who is banished by her uncle, Duke Frederick, and seeks refuge in the Forest of Arden. She disguises herself as Ganymede, a boy, and begins to teach a love-sick Orlando about the nature of love. In the forest, she meets other characters, including Celia, Oliver, and Touchstone, who experience their own romantic entanglements and personal growth. The play explores themes of love, exile, and the relationship between courtly life and the simpler life in the natural world.

This Murder Was Staged

By Patrick Greene & Jason Pazzarello

Directed by Gustavo Belotta

It's opening night of a brand-new mystery play, but just as the killer is about to be revealed, the body of the play's director falls on stage instead. In that moment, the theater becomes an active crime scene, and everyone from cast to crew to even the audience becomes a suspect. But how is the intrepid detective supposed to find the killer when everyone and their mother (literally) has a motive to want the demanding director gone?
