Education

Classes and workshops for all ages and skill levels

Learn the Art of Performance

NOVA Center offers a comprehensive education program designed to nurture talent, build confidence, and develop skills in the performing arts. Our experienced instructors provide professional training in a supportive, creative environment.

Whether you're a beginner exploring theatre for the first time or an experienced performer looking to refine your craft, we have a class for you. Many of these classes are available for outreach in your space. Reach out to our Youth Conservatory Director @ youth@novabillings.org for more information and pricing.
